FAQs

Are you open for commissions?

I will announce future commission openings on my Telegram channel.

How old do I have to be to commission or purchase from you?

You must be 18 years of age or older. I do not work with minors, even with parental permission.

Do you accept payment plans?

It depends. In some situations, I can accept a 50% down payment plan, to be paid off within a shorter timeframe.

Do you take commissions for just eyes?

I don't, sorry!

Can I send you materials for my commission?

Maybe! Please let me know during the submission process if you would like to send in fur.

Where do you get your materials?

I get my furs from Howl Fabrics and BigZ, and my mochi minky is usually sourced from Hopen Textiles. My eye mesh is computer mesh that I custom order from Alibaba.

What is your turn-around time?

Premades and in-stock items will ship within 3 business days of payment.

Commission times depend on the project, and are also affected by how many orders are in my queue. I do not keep a long queue, but I do work in order of payment received.

If you have a certain deadline that needs to be met, please let me know before commissioning. I will do my best to work with you!
