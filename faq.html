FAQs

What premades do you have available?

I will post new premades on this website here and my Telegram channel.

How do I commission you?

I do not take commissions at this time.

How old do I have to be to purchase from you?

You must be 18 years of age or older. I do not work with minors, even with parental permission.

Do you accept payment plans?

It depends. In some situations, I can accept a 50% down payment plan, to be paid off within a shorter timeframe.

Do you take commissions for just eyes?

I don't, sorry!

Where do you get your materials?

I get my furs from Howl Fabrics and BigZ, and my mochi minky is usually sourced from Hopen Textiles. My eye mesh is computer mesh that I custom order from Alibaba.

What is your turn-around time?

Premades and in-stock items will ship within 3-5 business days of payment.
